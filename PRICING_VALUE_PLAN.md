## Competitive Landscape

### National Platforms (for context)

| Platform | Pricing Model | Cost Range |
|----------|---------------|------------|
| **Thumbtack** | Pay-per-lead | $15-100+ per lead |

### Our Advantage
- **No per-lead fees** - Unlimited leads at flat rate
- **Local focus** - Not competing with providers 50 miles away
- **No bidding wars** - Fair visibility based on tier, not ad spend
- **Relationship-based** - We know the community

### Positioning Statement
> "One good job in Boerne pays for a year of Professional. No lead fees, no bidding wars, just local customers finding you."

## Pricing Psychology Tactics

### 1. Anchor High
Always show Featured first or make it visually prominent. Even if they don't buy it, $249 makes $79 feel reasonable.

### 2. Annual Discount
Offer 2 months free on annual plans:
- Starter: $39/mo → $390/yr (save $78)
- Professional: $79/mo → $790/yr (save $158)
- Featured: $249/mo → $2,490/yr (save $498)

This also improves cash flow and reduces churn.

### 3. ROI Calculator
Add a simple calculator to the pricing page:
> "If your average job is $_____, you only need _____ customers per year to pay for Professional."

Example: $500 average job → 2 jobs pays for a full year of Professional

### 4. Risk Reversal
- "Try Professional free for 14 days"
- "Not getting leads? We'll refund your first month"
- "Downgrade anytime, no questions asked"

### 5. Social Proof on Pricing Page
- "127 businesses trust Boerne's Handy Hub"
- "Join 23 other plumbers in your area"
- Testimonials from paid members
