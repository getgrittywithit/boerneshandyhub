**Canonical source of truth for pricing tiers, features, and visual treatment.**

Last updated: 2026-05-03. Status: v1 Launch.

## Tier Overview

| # | Tier | Price | What They Get | What We Get |
|---|------|-------|---------------|-------------|
| 0 | Unclaimed | Free (we add) | Basic NAP data, grayscale card | Full-looking directory |
| 1 | Claimed | Free (they claim) | Edit profile, logo, hours, social links | Email capture, accurate data |
| 2 | Verified | $29/mo | Enhanced card, 5 photos, do-follow backlink, priority placement | Recurring revenue |
| 3 | Verified+ | $67/mo | Everything above + rented website OR authority boost package | High-margin revenue, SEO authority |

Annual pricing available:
- Verified: $260/year (~$22/mo, save $88)
- Verified+: $620/year (~$52/mo, save $184)

## Tier 0 — Unclaimed

**Cost:** Free (we add from public data)
**Question it answers:** "Does this business exist?"

### What they get
- Business name
- Phone number
- Address
- Category placement

### Visual treatment (intentionally incomplete)
- Grayscale or placeholder photo (silhouette icon)
- Missing fields shown as gray dashes "—"
- "Profile not verified" tag
- Banner: "Are you the owner? Claim this listing free →"
- Sorts to bottom of category
- Muted card styling

### Strategic note
The empty profile next to filled-out competitors creates urgency. Shame factor drives claims.

## Tier 1 — Claimed (Free)

**Cost:** Free (they claim it)
**Question it answers:** "Here's our info."

### What they get (everything in Unclaimed, plus)
- 1 photo (logo)
- Business description
- Hours of operation
- Website link (no-follow)
- Social media links
- Edit access via dashboard

### Visual treatment
- Full color, normal card styling
- Subtle "Listed" tag
- Standard category sort position

### Strategic note
This tier exists to capture email. Once we have email, the upgrade nurture sequence begins.

## Tier 2 — Verified ($29/mo)

**Cost:** $29/month or $260/year
**Question it answers:** "I want to look professional and boost my SEO."

### What they get (everything in Claimed, plus)
- ✓ **Boerne Verified** badge on listing
- Enhanced "good looking" card design
- Up to 5 photos in gallery
- **Do-follow backlink** to their website (SEO value)
- Social media icon links
- Listed in up to 2 categories
- Priority placement above free listings
- "Request Quote" button

### Visual treatment
- Green "Boerne Verified" checkmark
- Slight accent border
- Sorts above all free listings

### Pricing
- Month-to-month: $29/mo
- Annual: $260/year (save $88)

### Strategic note
The do-follow backlink is real, immediate SEO value even before HandyHub has traffic. Lead with this in the pitch.

## Tier 3 — Verified+ ($67/mo)

**Cost:** $67/month or $620/year
**Question it answers:** "I want a complete local web presence."

### Startup: The Founder's Bundle
**$199 upfront** covers first 3 months + onboarding. After 90 days, moves to $67/mo.

Why 3 months minimum:
- Google takes 60-90 days to index and rank new pages
- Covers onboarding labor (photo QC, copy review, approval)
- Eliminates early churn before they see results

### What they get (everything in Verified, plus)

**Choose one track at onboarding:**

#### Track A: "Build My Presence" (No existing website)
- Rented website at `boerneshandyhub.com/business-name`
- Mobile-responsive, SEO-optimized template
- Custom QR code kit (decals, digital assets)
- We handle all tech; they fill out onboarding form

#### Track B: "Boost My Authority" (Has existing website)
- Premium do-follow backlink package
- Embeddable "Boerne Verified" badge for THEIR website
- Marketing kit (truck decals, social graphics, digital badge)
- Enhanced backlink from multiple pages

**Both tracks get:**
- Top-of-category placement (above Verified)
- "Boerne Verified" badge (enhanced version)
- Priority in quote request routing
- Up to 15 photos
- Listed in up to 4 categories
- Future: Newsletter inclusion, social features as audience grows

### Visual treatment
- Enhanced "Verified+" badge
- Elevated card styling
- Always sorts above Verified tier

### The Website (Track A)
- Subfolder structure: `boerneshandyhub.com/johns-painting`
- Why subfolder: Inherits domain authority, every backlink to their page strengthens the whole site
- Templates: 4 design-based options (Classic, Modern, Friendly, Bold)
- Content: Services, photos, hours, contact, testimonials
- Mobile-responsive, fast-loading

### Strategic note
The website is the v1 anchor. For businesses without a site, it's concrete value regardless of directory traffic. For businesses with a site, Track B provides SEO boost and trust signals.

## The Pitch Framework

### To the Free Prospect
"I've already built a profile for you. Claim it free so your info is accurate and you can add your logo."

### To the $29 Prospect
"You've got a great site. The Verified tier gives you a premium backlink from our growing local directory — that helps YOUR site rank higher in Boerne searches. Plus you look more professional than the free listings."

### To the $67 Prospect (No Website)
"You're busy in the field. For $67/mo, we build you a professional website, handle all the tech, and put you at the top of our directory. You just fill out a form and we do the rest."

### To the $67 Prospect (Has Website)
"Your site is solid, but imagine showing up twice on Page 1 — your site AND your HandyHub page. Plus you get our 'Boerne Verified' badge to put on your own site and truck. Double the hooks in the water."

## Operational Notes

### Onboarding Flow (Verified+)
1. Customer pays $199 Founder's Bundle
2. Automated email with onboarding form link
3. They submit: business info, photos, services, hours, bio
4. System drafts website/listing
5. Admin QC review + approve
6. Site goes live, customer notified
7. After 90 days, billing moves to $67/mo

### Moderation
- All new sites require approval before going live
- Edits to content fields trigger re-review
- Pre-checks: profanity filter, image moderation, required fields
- Admin can take any site offline instantly (kill switch)

### The 60-90 Day Pitch
"Google takes 60-90 days to index new pages. That's why we require a 3-month start — we don't want you judging results before the SEO kicks in."
